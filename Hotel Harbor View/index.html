    Welcome to Hotel Harbor view

    Welcome to the Awesome Hotel Harbor View! After the most fertile journey, you have now arrived to find generous accommodation in one of the best places in the beautiful southern city. Hotel Harbor View is blessed with a selection of scenery by nature. The hotel has a beautiful natural harbor to the south, with a beautiful view surrounded by blue beaches and large mountain ranges on the other side, as close as you can get. It offers spectacular views up to the south coast.

    Hotel Harbor View is blessed with view hand picked by nature her self. The hotel’s location ensures you enjoy this magnificent view from every point, be it the entrance/reception, the dinning area and certainly every bedroom, not forgetting the bathroom too! This is just one of the reasons why you should stay here, there are other reasons too like the spacious accommodation with all amenities at hand, the awesome cuisine which includes authentic Sri Lankan fare, the well stocked bar to relax and enjoy, the infinity swimming pool that merges with the mountain tops, the helpful and friendly staff and most importantly being close to all the major attractions, yet surrounded by an unmatched peace and quiet that every traveller yearns for.
